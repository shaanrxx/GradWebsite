ABOUT
Languages across the globe are disappearing at a distressing pace, with current predictions suggesting that up to 50% of the world's existing 7,000 languages might vanish by the century's end. A number of these languages are on the brink of extinction, with merely a few speakers left. The significance of this loss extends far beyond mere linguistic expression; it encompasses the disappearance of diverse worldviews, cultural identities, and repositories of knowledge.
This video project features the audio of 8 severely endangered languages from across the globe and highlights the beauty of their sound, music and song related to their culture and most importantly the need for cultural and linguistic diversity.
